Frequently Asked Questions

What is Availly?

Availly is a free-time sharing calendar app. Create a calendar of the time slots you're available, share it with friends, family, or colleagues, and let them propose meetings that fit your schedule.

How do I create a calendar?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, give the calendar a name, add the time slots you're free, and choose who to share it with. You can share by email invite or via a link.

How do meeting proposals work?

When someone views a calendar you've shared with them, they can pick a free time slot and send you a proposal. You'll get it in the app and can accept, decline, or suggest a different time.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app and select "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your profile, calendars, time slots, proposals, and invites. You can also email iclibera@gmail.com to request deletion.

Is Availly free?

Yes, Availly is free to download and use.

What data does Availly collect?

See our Privacy Policy for full details. In short: we collect only what's needed to run the app (account info, calendars, proposals, invites) and never sell your data.
